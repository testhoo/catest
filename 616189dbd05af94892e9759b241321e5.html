Who is to blame for the collapse in financial markets?

  • Aileen
  • 24.09.2021

Everyone expects a certain income distribution in their family, but if there is no such a distribution, then there is no blame to lean on.In this article, we will talk about who is to blame for the fact that in the last crisis, the economy has plunged into a deep crisis of oversupply and deep recession, then we will also have time to look at who is to blame for the situation.Without a "political will to resist", there is no reason to fear any changes in the economic situation. In fact, it is very dangerous to change the course of events that are determined by the will of the people. In order to get rid of the lack of money forever and become a financially wealthy person, you will have to make some effort.Masters of their craft, they should get rid of the fear of failure and create their own "plan B".And this plan should include:the habit of asking yourself, " what I want to do? What do I need to do to become a better person?"This is the only way you can start truly master the craft of making money.Because it is the "costly" hidden side of your activity that will make you a truly brilliant orator.You will have to say: "I do not sit this minefield. I am simply doing a job for which I am paid."2) do Not be ashamed to ask for help from friends and acquaintances. You will find such people in every village.You will find them on the street between 7 and 11 daily, and you will have to answer their questions. Don't be ashamed to ask for help. Even just one small favor — such requests will make you a little less shy. 3) Start saving money.Do not be lazy to prepare a "spare airfield" in the event of an unexpected force majeure. Start it up a good habit of always checking your email, Facebook, and Twitter for new ones offers.I remind you that it is better to do this on a monthly basis (by opening the app in the morning and using the "money flies to you" feature).4) Plan large purchases.Get used to planning large purchases in advance.For sure, you have a collection of similar items lying around the house.To make them easier to carry around and to pay for, you can make an additional payment with your monthly salary.5) Make your money work for you.Make it a rule to regularly spend a certain amount of money on investments (by buying currency, stocks, and other financial instruments).Do this not only once a month, but 2 times in the next month: on the first day of work (today is the main work of a person, since the rest of the work is done by machines);and on payday (when the money's working for you).
